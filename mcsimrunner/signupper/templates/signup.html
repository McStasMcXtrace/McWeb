PAN-LEARNING TERMS & DATA COLLECTION POLICY
When you create an account at pan-learning.org you can rest assured that we will not pass on your personal information to third parties. If you are enrolled in one or several courses at pan-learning, the designated teachers and students of those courses at pan-learning.org will have access to see your registration data. If you have enrolled in one or or several courses at pan-learning, the designated teacher(s) of those courses will have access to track your progress within the course including attempts and grades in quizzes. Fellow students will not have access to track your progress including attempts and grades in quizzes. By signing up to pan-learning you certify your acceptance of the above terms and data collection policy. As a pan-learning user, you may at any time contact us to request information on / removal of your user records.
